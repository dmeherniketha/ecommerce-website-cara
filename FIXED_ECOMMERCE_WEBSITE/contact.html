Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in their original condition. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs duties and taxes may apply.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.
